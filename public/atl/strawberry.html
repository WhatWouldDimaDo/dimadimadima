Sunday May 3 · Full Day Plan
How the day looks

🍓 Southern Belle Farm — May 3 · Leave at 8 AM

8:00 AM
Leave 30306
I-75 South via the Connector. Pack carriers, cooler, snacks, extra clothes. ~45 min drive.
8:50 AM
Breakfast — Truett's Grill, McDonough
1950s Chick-fil-A diner by founder Truett Cathy. Extended menu, familiar food for the toddlers, high chairs, fast. 1785 Jonesboro Rd, McDonough.
9:30 AM
Head to Noah's Ark Animal Sanctuary — 15 min
712 LG Griffin Rd, Locust Grove. Opens 10am. Grab coffee and stall for 30 min if early.
10:00 AM
Noah's Ark opens — paved paths, stroller-accessible
Tigers, bears, wolves, lions on paved self-guided paths. $5/person. Kids 12 & under free. Plan ~2 hrs.
12:15 PM
Leave Noah's Ark → Southern Belle (15 min)
Buy activity tickets online before you arrive — $17.95/adult. Gate price is $20.95.
12:45 PM
Arrive Southern Belle — get organized
Park, load carriers, grab water. Gates open at 1pm sharp — be at the entrance ready to go.
1:00 PM
Gates open — straight to the strawberry field
Leave strollers at the edge. Baby carriers in. Toddlers will eat half of what they pick. That's the plan.
2:15 PM
Barnyard activities
Cow train, jumping pillows, Barnstormer slide, petting barn, pig races. Toddlers could run this for 90 min.
3:30 PM
Country Market + Mimi's ice cream
Homemade strawberry ice cream is famous. Pick up jams, fresh produce for the week. Load cooler.
4:00 PM
Head home — I-75 North
Home by ~4:45 PM. Plenty of time before bedtime. Berries straight into the fridge — don't wash until ready to eat.

Logistics
What to bring

👶 For the kids

  • 🎒 Baby carrier (essential — strollers can't enter rows)
  • 👗 Change of clothes — strawberries stain everything
  • 🧴 Sunscreen — field is fully exposed
  • 👒 Sun hats for toddlers
  • 🧃 Extra snacks and water bottles

✅ Day-of checklist

  • 📱 Check southernbellefarm.com banner morning of May 3 — fields can close early if picked out
  • 📞 Call 770-288-2582 morning-of to confirm field is open
  • 🎟️ Buy activity tickets online — saves $3/person vs. gate
  • 👟 Closed-toe shoes — expect dirt, possibly damp rows
  • 💳 Card or cash — gate + market
  • 🧊 Cooler in the car — berries in summer heat = mush
  • ⏰ Last entry to u-pick patch is 5pm (1 hr before 6pm close) — plan to be in the field no later than 4:30pm

🚗 Getting there from 30306

  • 🛣️ Head to I-75 South via the Downtown Connector
  • 🚦 Exit 216 (Hwy 155 / McDonough) → ~5 min east to Turner Church Rd
  • ⏱️ ~45 min, 35 miles
  • 🅿️ Free parking, large lot on arrival
  • 📍 Get directions from 30306 →

🍓 Picking tips

  • 🔴 Deep red all the way up, firm but gives slightly — that's ripe
  • 🚫 White shoulders = not ready; soft/mushy = overripe
  • ✂️ Twist and pull, don't yank the plant
  • 🧺 One gallon fills a flat of quarts — more than you think
  • 😄 Toddlers will eat 30% of what they pick. That's fine.
