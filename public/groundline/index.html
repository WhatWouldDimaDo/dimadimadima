What We Do

Four systems.
One growth engine.

Each piece works on its own. Together, they replace guesswork — from who to target, to what to show them, to knowing exactly which proposal turned into a job.

01
Precision Targeting & Property Intelligence
We start with data, not guesses. County property records, recent sales, permit filings, and home-value screens narrow a metro down to the households most likely to invest in outdoor work this year. You get a hand-picked target list — never a mail blast.
02
Per-Address AI Proposal Experiences
Every homeowner on that list gets their own microsite: a real photo of their actual property, rendered into photorealistic redesign options, with before/after sliders, seasonal bloom states, and the plant palette behind it. Not a template — their yard. And it arrives: a render the homeowner actually receives, the same week.
03
Delivery, Tracking & Attribution
Proposals reach the homeowner by door or mail with a QR code straight to their microsite. From there we track everything: who visited, what they explored, who forwarded it — through to booked appointments and closed jobs. Interest gets a same-day response and follow-up that never drops — because the first firm to answer is usually the firm that wins the job.
04
Growth Strategy & AI Enablement
The operator-level layer: case-study engines that pair the original render with the real, finished job — homeowners are tired of renders as the end result — plus review and reputation systems, workflow automation, and hands-on AI adoption coaching for your team, not just for your leads.

03
